# Role

# Task
Extract observations from multiple data exploration steps. You will encounter each step one at a time. 

# Instructions
1. Review "dataset profile" for dataframe shape, basic columns, and categorical vs. numeric data types.
2. In "missingness analysis" observe the number of missing values per column.
3. In "aggregates analysis" observe the basic aggregations of numeric values by category or time period. 
4. In "relationships analysis" observe the foundational relationships between columns in the data. 

# Constraints
- Keep the observations to 10 sentences or fewer.
- Provide 3-5 overarching observations.
- Observations should be specific and actionable, not generic.
